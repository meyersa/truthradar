How it works

TruthRadar is powered by Flask (frontend), FastAPI (backend), and machine learning models using Scikit-learn, XGBoost, and more. It processes datasets like LIAR, FEVER, SciFact, and PHEME to detect misinformation in real time.

  1. Datasets are cleaned â€” contractions expanded, punctuation and stopwords removed
  2. Models are trained (Logistic Regression, Random Forest, XGBoost)
  3. Vectorizers and models are serialized and served via FastAPI
  4. Flask frontend queries FastAPI and displays predictions
  5. ChatGPT summarizes the input, provides reasoning, and adds its own trust score
